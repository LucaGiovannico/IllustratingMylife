How i do my work

Software

My works are created with Adobe illustrator so that the illustration is created in a vector manner and there are no problems when enlarging.

Inspiration

Having had a passion for anime, manga and cartoons since I was a child, I take inspiration above all from the latter.

Colors

I use bright colors so that they immediately stand out and give a feeling of lightness and carefreeness to whoever is looking at it.
